PackMen

Exercise 2: In this exercise you have two buttons "Add PackMan" and "Start Game". When you click the button "Add PackMan", a new packman will appear in the website. When you click the button "Start Game" the packman will move to a random direction with a random speed. You can add several packmen in a similar way.

Bus Stoping

Exercise 3: This exercise shows how a marker moves through bus stops from MIT to Harvard. The marker moves from one stop to another after certain quantity of seconds.
